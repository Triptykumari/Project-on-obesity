Obesity

Obesity is defined as the increase in body weight due to an increase in the amount of adipose tissue (body fat). Obesity is caused by imbalance of energy and causes a greater burden on public health. It is caused by overtime by unhealthy food choices and lack of exercise. Obesity is a precursor to obesity. Our project is about to provide information and possible treatment to obese patients. The purpose of the move is to bring about behavioral changes and move on to a more physically active lifestyle. Overweight and obesity is a big problem today. We can say that mass index (BMI) over 25 is overweight, and over 30 is overweight.
